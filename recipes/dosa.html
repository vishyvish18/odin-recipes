Dosa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal
  • 3 cup sona masuri rice
  • 1/2 tsp methi
  • 1 cup beaten rice
  • oil for roasting dosa
  • salt
  1. Step 1: soak the urad dal, rice and methi in a bowl(seperate bowls) for 5 hours
  2. Step 2: blend the urad dal to make batter
  3. Step 3: add the beaten rice to blender and grind to paste
  4. Step 4: transfer batter to the bowl and mix
  5. Step 5: cover and ferment for 12 hours
  6. Step 6: take the batter in a different bowl and add salt
  7. Step 7: pour oil on tawa and make the dosa
